Glide

Glide our little dude down through an infinite stream of objects in this addictive new endless runner. Tap to move left and right.

- Fast games with simple controls. Have a spare couple of minutes? Pick up and have a quick play!
- Awesome retro 8-bit graphics and sound effects.
- Three different game speeds - slow, normal and fast mean you can play a speed that suits you best!
- Power ups! - duck under pipes or jump over them. If all else fails, make the gap bigger! Simply tap the icon or flick your phone to activate!

Ski Panic

From the creators of glide, comes an timeless classic skiing game. Suit up; you're going for the ride of your life!

Ski Panic is a fast paced, action packed, addictive skiing game which will really test your reflexes! Avoid trees and rocks as you fly down the slopes at break neck speed. Simply tap left and right to move.

The retro 2D look, along with the nostalgic 8 bit sound track and simple controls will give you the opportunity to relive your youth, back when only one thing was important - beating your high score!
